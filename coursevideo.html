Basic of Space Flight — Introduction
By Prof. Saptarshi Kolay  ·  Week I, Lesson 1  ·  31 mins  ·  1,240 views
About this lesson
In this introductory lesson, we explore the fundamentals of space flight — from the basics of orbital mechanics to understanding how spacecraft are designed to survive the harsh environment of space. We'll cover the solar system structure, the concept of microgravity, and how radiation affects spacecraft systems. This lesson lays the groundwork for everything that follows in this course. ...more
We'll also touch on Kepler's laws of planetary motion, the history of human spaceflight, and an overview of major space agencies and private players shaping the future of aerospace. By the end of this lesson, you'll have a clear picture of the landscape of modern space exploration.
